Title: Market News Sentiment Explainer
Document Type: Market Education
Project: EquityGuard

News sentiment means the overall tone of recent news about a company or stock. It can be positive, neutral, negative, or mixed.

Why News Matters
News can influence investor expectations. A stock may move because of:
- earnings updates
- regulatory issues
- acquisitions
- management changes
- legal issues
- dividend updates
- analyst reports
- sector-wide developments

How EquityGuard Uses News
EquityGuard checks news sentiment to explain whether recent headlines may be connected to price movement or volume changes.

Negative News
Negative news can increase the risk score, especially when combined with falling price, unusual volume, or weak technical signals.

Positive News
Positive news may reduce some risk, but it does not guarantee future performance.

No News Found
If no relevant news is found after unusual price movement or volume, EquityGuard should explain that the cause is unclear and treat the result as uncertain.
