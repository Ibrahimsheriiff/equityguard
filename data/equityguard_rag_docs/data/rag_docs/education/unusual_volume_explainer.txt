Title: Unusual Volume Explainer
Document Type: Market Education
Project: EquityGuard

Volume means the number of shares traded during a period. Unusual volume means trading activity is much higher or lower than normal.

Why Volume Matters
Volume can show how much attention a stock is receiving. A price move with high volume may be more meaningful than a price move with low volume.

Possible Reasons for Unusual Volume
- company announcement
- earnings result
- broker upgrade or downgrade
- sector news
- macroeconomic news
- institutional trading
- market rumours
- index rebalancing

How EquityGuard Uses Volume
If volume is unusually high, EquityGuard should investigate further by checking news, earnings calendar, and company documents.
If volume is unusually low, EquityGuard should treat the signal carefully because price movement may be less reliable.

Important Limitation
Unusual volume does not always mean something bad happened. It is a trigger for investigation, not a conclusion.
